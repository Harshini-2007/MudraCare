üí™ Mudras for Body Pains (Back, Leg, Hand, Sprains)

Vayu Mudra

Purpose: For leg pain, sprains, spasms, and sciatica.

  • Bend index finger to base of thumb.
  • Press gently with thumb.
  • Other fingers extended.
Varun Mudra

Purpose: For hand joint pain, inflammation.

  • Touch little finger tip to thumb.
  • Do not press hard.
  • Others extended.
Back Mudra

Purpose: Neck and back tension relief.

  • Right: Thumb + middle + little finger tips.
  • Left: Thumb + index finger tips.
Ganesha Mudra

Purpose: Opens chest, eases fatigue.

  • Hook fingers at chest, palms opposing.
  • Pull gently while exhaling.
